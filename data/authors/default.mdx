## Hello, Y'all! I'm Seonghwa Lee (aka MartianLee)

I'm a software engineer currently at **UJET**, where I've been working for 2 years. I thrive in remote-first environments and have built strong habits around async communication and self-driven productivity.

I believe great software is built through **collaboration and iteration**. My goal is always to be the kind of engineer teammates can rely on — someone who ships and communicates clearly.

I think the role of an engineer is fundamentally shifting. It's no longer just about writing code — it's about deeply understanding what people actually need (the kind of thinking that used to belong to PMs), and making wise technical decisions that serve those needs. The engineer who can bridge that gap is the one who creates the most impact.

That's also why I'm focused on becoming a **10x engineer powered by AI** — not by working harder, but by working smarter. I see AI as the most powerful lever available to engineers right now, and I'm actively building habits and workflows around it to multiply my own output and impact.
